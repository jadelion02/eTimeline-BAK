關於作者  

Raymond 是第3代基督徒，他在移居加拿大之前在青少年時期接受了基督。他在加受了土木工程的教育，並註冊為專業工程師{目前為退休成員}。畢業後，他在多倫多的一家建築諮詢公司工作了幾年，然後被阿爾伯塔省政府勞工部安全與服務部的建築標準機構聘為建築標準官員。後來，他加入了聯邦政府消防局部門成為高級工程師。他的工作涉及為加拿大政府財政部起草安全和建築政策和法規，管理聯邦政府擁有或租賃的建築物。他代表加拿大財政部檢查加拿大海外大使館和世界博覽會的加拿大館等其他建築結構是否符合加拿大標準。他是保險商實驗室(ULC)和加拿大標準協會(CSA)等標準編寫組織委員會成員。 2011年3月，他被加拿大總督大衛約翰斯頓授予消防模範服務獎章。他在退休前曾擔任聯邦消防局副局長和代理總工程師。

About the author

Raymond is a 3rd generation Christian, who accepted Christ in his teenage years before emigrating to Canada. He received his education in civil engineering and is registered as a professional engineer (currently as a retired member). After graduation, he worked for a consulting firm for several years in Toronto before joining the Alberta Labour General Safety & Services Division - Building Standards Branch as a Building Standard Officer. Later, he joined the Office of the Fire Commissioner with the federal government and held the title of Senior Engineer. His work involved the drafting of safety and construction policies and regulations, governing buildings and structures owned and leased by the federal government, for the Treasury Board of Canada. On behalf of the Treasury Board of Canada, He inspected overseas Canadian embassies and other building structures such as Canadian Pavilions at the World Expositions for compliance of Canadian standards. He was a committee member of standard writing organizations such as Underwriters’ Laboratories and Canadian Standard Association. He was awarded the Fire Services Exemplary Service Medal from the Governor General of Canada David Johnston in March 2011. He held the positions of Deputy Federal Fire Commissioner and Acting Chief Engineer before his retirement.
