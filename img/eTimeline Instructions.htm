聖經中名字

“聖經中名字”包含聖經中提到的名字。 大約有 3,000 名字記錄在聖經中。目前，其中約有 1/4 的名字已存儲在數據庫中。 但因不同版本的聖經或會有不同的名字與寫法，其中也會有其他版本中沒有出現的名字。 另外，多 人可以有相同的名字。

英文數據庫中的名稱主要取自詹姆斯國王版本欽定本聖經（KJV），而中文數據庫中的名稱則取自中文和合本（CUV）。

如要在數據庫中搜索名字，請在屏幕頂部附近的搜索欄中輸入名字。 當你輸入名字時，名字列表將被縮短以顯示與你的輸入匹配的結果相同。 如果光標懸停在任何鏈接的文本上，箭頭光標將變成手形指針。 單擊鏈接文本將會打開關聯的網頁。 例如，當光標位於聖經經文上時，將出現”藍字聖經”(英文經文)和”聖經網關”(中文經文)中相應的經文。 單擊鏈接的文本將會打開"藍字聖經"。 "藍字聖經"面板將出現在前面 BibleGateway 經文面板，但它們的經文會略有重叠。 為了能夠閱讀後面"聖經網關"的面板，當你將光標滑動到"聖經網關" BibleGateway 後面的面板時，前面板將會消失。 單擊"(CUV)More"鏈接將打開"聖經網關" BibleGateWay 聖經。 有時，"聖經網關" BibleGateWay 無法打開相應版本的聖經，在這種情況下，你可能需要手動查找中文和合本(CUV)聖經。

如果你使用的瀏覽器(即 Chrome 瀏覽器)，另一種進行搜索的方法是使用語音識別功能。 從屏幕右上角附近拉下列表中選擇語言。 選擇語言後，單擊綠色.麥克風圖標 。 綠色麥克風圖標會變成紅色並開始閃爍，同時會詢問你授予權限使用你的麥克風設備。 如果獲得許可，請對著麥克風清楚地說出你要搜索的名字。 一旦名字出現在搜索欄上，你可以單擊閃爍的紅色圖標來停止麥克風。

如果數據庫中存在與口述姓名匹配的名字，請按 Enter 鍵激活搜索以縮短搜索列表。 如果沒有匹配，它則不會顯示任何的名單。 這意味著口語名字的拼寫無法在數據庫中找到匹配項，因為發現不匹配的情況並不罕見。 因為聖經中的一些名字並不是日常使用的常用名字。 例如，Rebekah 的現代拼寫是 Rebecca, 聖經中的“亞力山大”可能就是現代的”亞歷山大”。 因此，輸入姓名比使用語音識別功能更準確。

On a mobile device without an "Esc" key, pressing the Previous key (usually a ◁ left arrow) will achieve the same result.

eGenealogy of Jesus Settings

The settings panel can be activated by clicking the " ^ " (caret sign) button next to the "Home" button near the top of the screen . These settings alter the appearance of the network to provide a different view of the network. Other than the default setting, the most commonly used setting is the "Enable Approximation Visualization" setting, it provides the viewer a different perspective of the same network. Once a new setting is selected, press the "Reset" button to display the new layout of the network. There are two anchor points in the network, namely Adam and Jesus. When a box next to them is checked, the anchor point will be fixed or stayed stationary relative to the screen when the "Reset" button is pressed. If the box is not checked, the anchor point is allowed to float freely. The Overlap Number, from 0 to 1 with an increment of 0.2, denotes the degree of overlap of links permitted in the network.

The "Hierarchical Visualization" enables you to view the network in either a vertical and horizontal arrangements. There are four built-in visualization displays for the network. The "Stabilization" function suppresses the display while calculation is being performed in the background. Once the calculation is complete, the new network will be displayed.

To view the functions listed in the drop-down panel, press the " + " (plus sign) located near the bottom left-hand corner of the settings panel. Pressing the " - " (minus sign) will close the drop-down panel. To close the settings panel, press the " x " (cross sign) located on the top right-hand corner of the settings panel.

The Unstructured Genealogy consists of other indirect descendants mentioned in the Bible such as Moses, Aaron and others. To hide and unhide this layer of genealogy, toggle the checkbox titled "Hide Unstructured Genealogy".

Names in the Bible

"Names in the Bible" contains names that are mentioned in the Bible. Currently, about 1/4 of the names are in the database out of the approximately 3,000 unique names. Different versions of Bible contain names that do not appear in other versions, in addtional to the spelling of names are slightly different from other versions. Also, names are not unique, meaning that multiple persons could have the same name.

The names in the English database are taken mainly from the King James version (KJV) of the Bible, whereas the names in the Chinese database are taken from the Chinese Union version (CUV).

To conduct a search of the database for names, type the name in the search bar near the top of the screen. As you type, the name list will be shortened to show the result matching your input. If the cursor is hovered over any linked text, the arrow cursor will turn into a hand pointer. Clicking the linked texts will open the associated web page. For example, when the cursor is over the Bible verse, the respective verse in the Blue Letter Bible (English verse) and the BibleGateway (Chinese verse) will appear. Clicking the linked text will open Blue Letter Bible. The Blue Letter Bible panel will appear on top of the BibleGateway verse panel but will be offset slightly . In order to be able to read the panel behind, the front panel will disappear when you slide the cursor to the BibleGateway panel behind. Clicking the "(CUV) More" link will open the BibleGateWay Bible. Occassionally, the BibleGateWay does not open the appropriate version of the Bible, in that case you may need to manually locate the CUV Bible.

Another way to conduct a search is to use the voice recognition function if you are using a compatible browser (i.e. Chrome Browser). Select the language from the dropdown list near the top-right hand corner of the screen. Once the language is selected, click on the green microphone icon . The green microphone icon will turn red and started to blink , and at the same time you will be asked to grant permission to use the microphone of your device. If permission is granted, speak clearly into the microphone the name you are search for. You can click the blinking red icon to stop the microphone once a name appears on the search bar.

If there is a name in the database matching the spoken name, press the Enter key to activate the search to shorten the search list. If there is no match, nothing will appear in the short list. It means that the spelling of the spoken name cannot find a match in the database as it is not unusual to find a mismatch. As some of the names in the Bible are not common names used on a daily basis. For example, the modern spelling of Rebekah is Rebecca; Judah could be Juda. Therefore, typing in the name is more accurate than using the voice recognition function.

To start a new search, delete the name in the search bar or click the red cancel icon near the right side of the search bar.
